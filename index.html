June Shaw

Lifelong Learner

Starting out at Colorado State, I got my ticket to software development world with a CS degree. A recent graduate of a Full Stack program, I was able to 'level up' my skills. It's up to me to keep it going with all the vast dev community resources!

Why Software?

I have always loved the logical: math, science, and software. Writing software is like watching the evolution of an idea into a product that improves a process or experience, and hopefully fills in a gap that you didn't even know was there. Magic.

What else?

My family and friends, Sonny the golden retriever, hiking, bluegrass, learning to play the guitar, and an occasional trip to a beach.
